zer0_divide

first-ever EP from the x3nyth project - released 21 Dec 2023

zer0_divide is the debut EP from the x3nyth project.

You can download for free and stream zer0_divide here.

It's a concept EP: It tells the story of a newly awakened soul coming to terms with the universe around them.

~ Expanded lore for our conceptual stuff is coming to the wiki!

trivia

This record was the first official x3nyth release.

zer0_divide's cover art was designed by myself, using the noodle software.
