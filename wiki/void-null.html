void/null

experimental record for the x3nyth project - released 12 July 2026


cover art by rav3ndust

void/null is the second full-length record from the x3nyth project.

You can purchase and download void/null here (bandcamp) and here (subvert).

Like autosynchronicity, this is a concept record, and picks up where the last one left off. It tells the story of a universe in decay.

A small series of books are in the works telling the story that this record describes in audio. I'll be including more details on this soon on this page.

~ Expanded lore for our conceptual stuff is coming to the wiki!

trivia

The tracks on this record have been in production since May 2025. After the last record, I had a clear vision of a story I wanted to tell and sounds I wanted to explore. This record is just as experimental, and takes a more ambient and darker approach in its sonic landscapes than most tracks on the previous record, matching the concept of its story.

"static rain (distortion field II)" is dedicated to the memory of my late sister, Static.

void/nulls's cover art was designed by me. It was drawn in noodle.

The runic symbols you see at the top of the cover art spell out "x3nyth". These are Elder Futhark runes.
