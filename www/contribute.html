the obligatory contribution page

i would never say no to that cash money, kids

If you think I'm doing a good job on stuff and want to throw me a little cryptocurrency or a bit of USD or something, that is really nice of you and always appreciated. I should note that anything I make on this site will be put towards this site, as well as my other projects, to help them grow into something that I think people might enjoy. This could possibly include some GNU/Linux related videos, text walkthroughs, and other helpful things that I can think of.

What if I Don't Have Money

You don't need to worry about contributing financially if you don't want to or don't have the extra funds. Consider contributing some time to some of my free software projects on my Github or volunteer some time to helping other free software developers continue to make their projects amazing.

There are lots of ways you can contribute, too! You don't need to be a web designer or a programmer to be able to help out the free software community. You can help write great documentation for your favorite projects, help out with translating them into other languages, or even just spreading the word to others about your favorite free software projects. These are all great and helpful ways you can aid other users as well as the developers in making free software projects even better.
