homemade pizza

who doesn't love a good pizza?

Ah, pizza. One of the meals held as a favorite by many around the world, including myself. This is a simple, yet delicious recipe for homemade pizza that you're sure to enjoy as much as we do.

Note that we call for pepperoni, onions, and peppers in this recipe as toppings for the pizza, but you can omit or add whatever toppings you want!

ingredients

  • 2 1/2 teaspoon active dry yeast (1 packet)
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • 2 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 1/2 cup diced onions
  • 1/2 cup diced bell pepper (your choice of color)
  • Optional: Italian seasoning or oregano for extra flavor, if desired

cooking instructions

  1. In a small bowl, combine your warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for 5-10 minutes, until it becomes frothy.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine your flour and salt. Make and 'well' in the center, and add in the yeast mixture and the olive oil.
  3. Begin to mix until a dough forms. Knead it on a floured surface for 5-7 minutes until it is smooth and 'elastic' feeling.
  4. Place the dough in a lightly-oiled bowl, covering it with a clean kitchen towel. Let it rise in a warm place for about an hour.
  5. Preheat your oven to 475 degrees Fahrenheit. When the dough is risen, roll it out into your desired shape and thickness.
  6. Spread your pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  7. Now, sprinkle your shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  8. Add your sliced pepperoni, diced onions, and diced peppers as you desire on top of the pizza.
  9. If you wish, sprinkle your Italian seasoning or oregano over the top of the pizza now, as well.
  10. Transfer the pizza into the oven on a pizza sheet, and let it bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown.
  11. When it's done cooking, remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Slice it in your desired proportions, and serve immediately. Enjoy!

Homemade pizza is a fun and infinitely customizable meal for the whole family. Pick and choose some different toppings each time you make it, or go with a basic pepperoni that everyone is sure to love. It's always something we love cooking, and we hope this simple recipe helps you make some tasty homemade pizza of your own, as well!
